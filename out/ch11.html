Geographic Levels

Geographic levels could be different between different countries. While some countries have 3 levels of geographic zones, others may have more or less levels of geographic zones. A typical setting contains the following levels

  1. Nation

  2. State

  3. Provence

  4. District

Note that the top level of the geographic zone for any country should represent the nation while the lowest level should be where the smallest possible addressable location in the country. Only lowest level of geographic zones will be allowed to have service delivery units.(need to explain this better)
