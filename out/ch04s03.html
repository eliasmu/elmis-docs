Authorize a requisition

While a store man can initiate a requisition, another user at the facility could have a more powerful permission to authorize the request that is coming from the store man. A senior store or a person in charge of the health facility will be able to review the requisition initiated and submitted by the store man.

Once the authorization is completed, the request will be sent to the first supervisory node (typically a district) for approval
