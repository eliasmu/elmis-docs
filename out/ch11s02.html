Facility Type

Facility type is the type of logistics facility in that will be recorded in the system. While the most common facility types that come to mind are service delivery units like health centers and hospitals, the facility types for the purposes of logistics management could also contain warehouses at the state level, Provence logistics units and district warehouses (if they are available)
