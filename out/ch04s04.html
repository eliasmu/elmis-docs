Approval of a requisition

A district user will be able to navigate to requisitions -> Approval page to be able to approve the requisition.

Once at this page, he/she will be able to select the requisition which they would like to approve. by clicking the requisition of interest, the district supervisor will be able to open the details of the requisition and be able to view what the facility had requested.
